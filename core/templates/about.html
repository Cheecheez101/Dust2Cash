Our story

Solving the "dust" problem for everyday crypto users

Scattered fractions of coins accumulate on exchanges, wallets, and DeFi rails, yet most people have no practical way to unlock them. Dust2Cash aggregates these balances securely, converts them at institutional FX rates, then delivers instant payouts to bank accounts or mobile money.

We are headquartered in Nairobi with partners in Lagos, Kampala, and Johannesburg. Our in-house ops team runs 24/7 to keep conversions fast, compliant, and human-friendly.
